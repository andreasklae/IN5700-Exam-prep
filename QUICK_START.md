## Your First Steps

### 1. Welcome Dashboard (Current View)
- See your overall progress
- Check your points and streak
- Choose a quick action to get started

### 2. Start Learning
Click on **"Continue Learning"** or **"Topics"** in the navigation to:
- Explore all 7 topics
- Read through subtopics
- Mark them as complete to earn points

### 3. Practice with Flashcards
Click on **"Flashcards"** to:
- Select a topic or study all
- Flip cards to see answers
- Track your review progress

### 4. Test Yourself
Click on **"Quiz"** to:
- Take a quick 5-question quiz
- Or challenge yourself with 10 questions
- See detailed explanations after

### 5. Track Progress
Click on **"Progress"** to:
- View your statistics
- See topic mastery
- Check quiz performance
- View study activity calendar

### 6. Unlock Achievements
Click on **"Achievements"** to:
- See all available achievements
- Track which ones you've unlocked
- Aim for 100% completion

## Study Strategy Recommendation

### Week 1: Learn
- Read through all topics in Topic Explorer
- Mark subtopics as complete
- Goal: 100% topic coverage

### Week 2: Reinforce
- Review flashcards daily
- Focus on topics you find challenging
- Goal: Review all flashcards 2-3 times

### Week 3: Test
- Take topic-specific quizzes
- Review explanations for wrong answers
- Re-read related subtopics
- Goal: 80%+ average on all topics

### Week 4: Master
- Take mixed quizzes daily
- Practice explaining concepts out loud
- Focus on weak areas
- Goal: Consistently score 90%+

## Gamification Tips

- **Maintain Your Streak**: Study every day, even if just for 10 minutes
- **Points System**:
  - +5 points per subtopic read
  - +2 points per flashcard reviewed
  - +10 points per correct quiz answer
  - +50 bonus for perfect quiz score
- **Achievements**: Complete challenges to unlock all 8 achievements

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can:
- Close and reopen the browser - your progress persists
- Study across multiple sessions
- Track long-term improvement

**Warning**: Clearing browser data will reset your progress!
